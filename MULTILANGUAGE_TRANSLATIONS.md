## HOW TO USE TRANSLATIONS IN GOOGLE PLAY STORE

### Step 1: Add Language
1. Go to **Google Play Console** â†’ Your App
2. Go to **Store listing** section
3. Click **"Manage languages and translations"**
4. Click **"Add language"**
5. Select desired language (e.g., Spanish)

### Step 2: Fill Translation
For each language:
1. **App name**: Keep same or translate
2. **Short description**: Paste translated version
3. **Full description**: Paste translated version
4. **Screenshots**: Can keep same or localize
5. **Feature graphic**: Can keep same

### Step 3: Save & Publish
1. Review all fields
2. Click **Save**
3. Same APK works for all languages

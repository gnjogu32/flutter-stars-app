## STEP-BY-STEP UPLOAD PROCESS

### STEP 1: Create Google Play Developer Account

**Duration**: 30 minutes - 1 hour

1. Go to: https://play.google.com/console
2. Sign in with your **Google account**
3. Click **"Create account"**
4. Pay **$25 one-time fee** (credit/debit card required)
5. Complete your **developer profile**:
   - Developer name
   - Contact email
   - Website (optional)
   - Phone number
6. Accept terms
7. Wait for account verification (usually immediate, sometimes a few hours)

**You'll need**:
- Google account
- $25 payment method
- Phone number for verification

---

### STEP 2: Create Your App in Play Console

**Duration**: 5 minutes

1. In Play Console, click **"Create app"**
2. Fill in:
   - **App name**: `Starpage`
   - **Default language**: `English (United States)`
   - **App or game**: `App`
   - **Free or paid**: `Free`
3. Accept policies checkbox
4. Click **"Create app"**

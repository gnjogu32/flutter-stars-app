Privacy Policy - Starpage

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

Overview

Starpage ("we," "our," "us," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: Name, email, phone number, profile picture, bio
  • Content: Posts, comments, messages, photos, videos
  • Communications: Direct messages, feedback, support requests
  • Payment information: If applicable (handled by payment processors)
  • Device information: Device ID, hardware model, OS version

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, time spent
  • Device data: IP address, device type, browser type, operating system
  • Location data: Approximate location based on IP address (unless you disable)
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics

Third-Party Data

  • Social networks: If you connect social accounts
  • Third-party services: Information from Firebase, Cloud services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Service delivery: Providing app functionality
  • Communication: Sending notifications, support messages
  • Analytics: Understanding user behavior and improving services
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud and abuse
  • Legal: Complying with laws and regulations
  • Marketing: With your consent, send promotional content

3. Data Security

Security Measures

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure authentication with password hashing
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Firebase Security Rules for database access
  • Limited access to personal data (staff on need-to-know basis)

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until account deletion
  • Analytics data: Retained for 14 months
  • Deleted content: Permanently removed within 30 days
  • Backup data: Retained for 90 days after deletion

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Correct: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Export: Download your personal data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke permissions for data use

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@starpage.org

6. Children's Privacy

  • Starpage is not intended for users under 13 years old
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • If we discover such data, we delete it immediately
  • Parents/guardians concerned should contact: privacy@starpage.org

7. Data Transfers

  • Data is primarily stored in the United States
  • We comply with data protection laws
  • Transfers follow standard contractual clauses
  • International users consent to data transfers by using the app

8. Cookies & Tracking

  • Web version uses cookies to improve experience
  • You can disable cookies in browser settings
  • Analytics cookies track usage patterns
  • Third-party cookies may be used for ads

9. Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you're a California resident, you have:

  • Right to know: What data we collect and use
  • Right to delete: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to opt-out: Opt-out of data sales/sharing
  • Right to non-discrimination: No discrimination for exercising rights

Submit requests to: privacy@starpage.org

10. Your EU Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you're an EU resident, you have:

  • Right to access: Receive your data
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing: Limit how we use data
  • Right to data portability: Receive data in portable format
  • Right to object: Oppose certain processing
  • Right to withdraw consent: Revoke permissions

EU Data Protection Officer: dpo@starpage.org

Major changes: We'll notify you via email or in-app notification.
